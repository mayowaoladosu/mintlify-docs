Regions determine where compute, databases, clusters, and networking resources run.

## Choosing a region

Pick a region based on:

- User latency.
- Data residency needs.
- Service availability.
- Cost.
- Proximity to related resources.

## Keep related resources close

For lower latency, keep application VMs, PostgreSQL databases, Kubernetes clusters, and load balancers in the same region when possible.

<Info>
  Available regions can differ by product and provider capacity.
</Info>
