These terms summarize expected use of LayerRail.

## Acceptable use

Do not use LayerRail to:

- Abuse networks or third-party systems.
- Send spam.
- Host malware.
- Mine cryptocurrency without explicit approval.
- Attack, scan, or disrupt other services.
- Violate applicable law.

## Customer responsibility

You are responsible for:

- Securing your accounts.
- Protecting API tokens and SSH keys.
- Configuring firewalls correctly.
- Maintaining your application code.
- Reviewing resource costs before scaling.

## Platform responsibility

LayerRail operates the console, provisioning workflows, and managed service surfaces for supported infrastructure products.
