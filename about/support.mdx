For product help, billing questions, and provisioning issues, contact:

```text
support@layerrail.com
```

## What to include

Include as much context as possible:

- Project ID.
- Resource ID.
- Resource type.
- Location.
- What you expected.
- What happened instead.
- Any relevant timestamps.

## Before contacting support

Check:

- The resource state in the console.
- The [LayerRail status page](https://status.layerrail.com).
- Firewall and DNS settings.
- Billing verification status.
