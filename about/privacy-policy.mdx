This page summarizes the information LayerRail needs to operate the service.

## Account information

LayerRail stores account information needed for authentication, support, billing, and auditability.

## Service data

LayerRail stores metadata about resources you create, such as names, IDs, locations, states, and configuration values.

## Secrets

Secrets such as tokens, passwords, and keys should be handled carefully. Do not send secrets in support messages unless specifically requested through a secure channel.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact:

```text
support@layerrail.com
```
